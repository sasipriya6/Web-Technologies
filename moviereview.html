Movie review

Movie name:Shershaah

Story:

Shershaah is the story of PVC awardee Indian soldier Capt. Vikram Batra, whose bravery and unflinching courage in chasing the Pakistani soldiers out of Indian territory contributed immensely in India finally winning the Kargil War in 1999.

Director:Vishnuvardhan

Release Date:12 August 2021

Rating:3.5 out of 5

Highlights:

  • The Letter Exchange
  • The Conversation Of Vikram And Sunny
  • The Proposal By Vikram
  • The “Unofficial” Wedding
  • “Ye Dil Mange More” Scene
  • The Cremation Scene

Drawback:

One drawback of this incessant spotlight around Vikram is the supporting characters not really being given the space to shine. Apart from Dimple.
