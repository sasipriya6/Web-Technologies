Venkateswara Temple, Tirumala:

The Sri Venkateswara Swami Temple is a Hindu temple in the hill town of Tirumala at Tirupati in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh, India.The Temple is dedicated to Venkateswara.a form of Vishnu, who is believed to have appeared on the earth to save mankind from trials and troubles of Kali Yuga. The temple is also known by other names like Tirumala Temple.The temple is run by TTD Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams.
